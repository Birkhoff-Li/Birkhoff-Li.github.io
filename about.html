About

Hiroshi Abe (阿部 寛) is a Japanese actor born on June 22, 1964 in Kanagawa Prefecture. He began his career as a model before transitioning to acting in the late 1980s.

Known for his tall stature (189cm) and distinctive presence, he has become one of Japan's most recognisable and respected actors across film, television, and stage.

About This Site

This tribute site is inspired by the legendary simplicity of Hiroshi Abe's official website at abehiroshi.la.coocan.jp — a site widely celebrated on the internet for its radical, uncompromising plainness.

No frameworks. No JavaScript. No web fonts. Just HTML and a small CSS file.
